Why I Made This Website

I made this website for golfers who want a simple way to track the rounds they play and the scores they shoot. Many golfers enjoy remembering where they have played and how they performed at each course. I wanted to create a place where those memories could be saved in a simple format. As someone who enjoys golf, I know it is fun to look back and see how your game has changed over time. This site gives users a basic tool for storing that information. It can also become a place to share progress with friends in the future. The website is meant to combine personal golf memories with simple score tracking.

Another reason for making this website is that golfers often have goals beyond just improving their scores. Some want to play famous courses, some want to beat their best round, and others want to build a collection of golf memories over time. This website can support all of those goals in a simple and organized way. It is not meant to be overly complicated or difficult to use. Instead, it gives golfers an easy starting point for keeping records. As the site grows, more features could be added to make it even more useful. Right now, the focus is on keeping it clean, simple, and helpful. That makes it a good beginner project and a practical site for golf fans.
